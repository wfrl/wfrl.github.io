Theorem God_is_essentially_God_like

Theorem. God_is_essentially_God_like
x ∈ G → ess G x
Proof
01. 1 ⊢ x ∈ G, hypo.
02. 2 ⊢ x ∈ A, hypo.
03. 1, 2 ⊢ A ∈ P, God_has_only_positive_properties 1 2.
04. 1, 2 ⊢ □(A ∈ P), axiom_iv 3.
05. ⊢ □(A ∈ P → ∀y. y ∈ G → y ∈ A),
  nec God_has_any_positive_property.
06. ⊢ □(A ∈ P) → □∀y. y ∈ G → y ∈ A, K 5.
07. 1, 2 ⊢ □∀y. y ∈ G → y ∈ A, subj_elim 6 4.
08. 1 ⊢ x ∈ A → □∀y. y ∈ G → y ∈ A, subj_intro 7.
09. 1 ⊢ ∀A. x ∈ A → □∀y. y ∈ G → y ∈ A, uq_intro 8.
10. 1 ⊢ x ∈ G ∧ ∀A. x ∈ A → □∀y. y ∈ G → y ∈ A, conj_intro 1 9.
11. ⊢ ess G x ↔ x ∈ G ∧ ∀A. x ∈ A → □∀y. y ∈ G → y ∈ A, ess_equi.
12. 1 ⊢ ess G x, rsubj_elim 11 10.
God_is_essentially_God_like. ⊢
  x ∈ G → ess G x, subj_intro 12.

Dependencies
The given proof depends on seven axioms:
K, axiom_i, axiom_iv, efq, eq_subst, lem, separation.
