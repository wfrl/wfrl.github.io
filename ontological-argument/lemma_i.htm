Theorem. lemma_i
(A → ¬B) → ¬(A ∧ B)
Proof
01. 1 ⊢ A → ¬B, hypo.
02. 2 ⊢ A ∧ B, hypo.
03. 2 ⊢ A, conj_eliml 2.
04. 1, 2 ⊢ ¬B, subj_elim 1 3.
05. 2 ⊢ B, conj_elimr 2.
06. 1, 2 ⊢ ⊥, neg_elim 4 5.
07. 1 ⊢ ¬(A ∧ B), neg_intro 6.
lemma_i. ⊢ (A → ¬B) → ¬(A ∧ B), subj_intro 7.
