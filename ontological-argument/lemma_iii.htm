Theorem. lemma_iii
¬(A ∧ ¬B) → A → B
Proof
01. 1 ⊢ ¬(A ∧ ¬B), hypo.
02. 2 ⊢ A, hypo.
03. 3 ⊢ ¬B, hypo.
04. 2, 3 ⊢ A ∧ ¬B, conj_intro 2 3.
05. 1, 2, 3 ⊢ ⊥, neg_elim 1 4.
06. 1, 2 ⊢ ¬¬B, neg_intro 5.
07. 1, 2 ⊢ B, dne 6.
08. 1 ⊢ A → B, subj_intro 7.
lemma_iii. ⊢ ¬(A ∧ ¬B) → A → B, subj_intro 8.

Dependencies
The given proof depends on two axioms:
efq, lem.
