Theorem. K_lemma_v
□A → □B → □(A ∧ B)
Proof
01. 1 ⊢ A, hypo.
02. 2 ⊢ B, hypo.
03. 1, 2 ⊢ A ∧ B, conj_intro 1 2.
04. 1 ⊢ B → A ∧ B, subj_intro 3.
05. ⊢ A → B → A ∧ B, subj_intro 4.
06. ⊢ □(A → B → A ∧ B), nec 5.
K_lemma_v. ⊢ □A → □B → □(A ∧ B), Kii 6.
