Theorem. if_God_exists_then_nec
(∃x. x ∈ G) → □∃y. y ∈ G
Proof
01. 1 ⊢ ∃x. x ∈ G, hypo.
02. 2 ⊢ x ∈ G, hypo.
03. 2 ⊢ ess G x, God_is_essentially_God_like 2.
04. 2 ⊢ x ∈ {x | x ∈ Univ ∧ ∀A. A ∈ P → x ∈ A}, eq_subst G_eq 2.
05. 2 ⊢ x ∈ Univ ∧ ∀A. A ∈ P → x ∈ A, sep_elim 4.
06. 2 ⊢ ∀A. A ∈ P → x ∈ A, conj_elimr 5.
07. 2 ⊢ NE ∈ P → x ∈ NE, uq_elim 6.
08. 2 ⊢ x ∈ NE, subj_elim 7 axiom_v.
09. 2 ⊢ x ∈ {x | x ∈ Univ ∧ ∀E. ess E x → □∃y. y ∈ E},
  eq_subst NE_eq 8.
10. 2 ⊢ x ∈ Univ ∧ ∀E. ess E x → □∃y. y ∈ E, sep_elim 9.
11. 2 ⊢ ∀E. ess E x → □∃y. y ∈ E, conj_elimr 10.
12. 2 ⊢ ess G x → □∃y. y ∈ G, uq_elim 11.
13. 2 ⊢ □∃y. y ∈ G, subj_elim 12 3.
14. 1 ⊢ □∃y. y ∈ G, ex_elim 1 13.
if_God_exists_then_nec. ⊢ (∃x. x ∈ G) → □∃y. y ∈ G,
  subj_intro 14.

Dependencies
The given proof depends on eight axioms:
K, axiom_i, axiom_iv, axiom_v, efq, eq_subst, lem, separation.
