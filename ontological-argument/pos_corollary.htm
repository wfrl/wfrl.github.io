Theorem. pos_corollary
¬◇A → □¬A
Proof
01. 1 ⊢ ¬◇A, hypo.
02. 2 ⊢ ¬□¬A, hypo.
03. 2 ⊢ ◇A, pos_fold 2.
04. 1, 2 ⊢ ⊥, neg_elim 1 3.
05. 1 ⊢ ¬¬□¬A, neg_intro 4.
06. 1 ⊢ □¬A, dne 5.
pos_corollary. ⊢ ¬◇A → □¬A, subj_intro 6.

Dependencies
The given proof depends on three axioms:
efq, lem, pos_equi.
