Theorem. sep_intro
u ∈ A → Q u → u ∈ {x | x ∈ A ∧ Q x}
Proof
01. 1 ⊢ u ∈ A, hypo.
02. 2 ⊢ Q u, hypo.
03. 1, 2 ⊢ u ∈ A ∧ Q u, conj_intro 1 2.
04. 1, 2 ⊢ u ∈ {x | x ∈ A ∧ Q x}, rsubj_elim separation 3.
05. 1 ⊢ Q u → u ∈ {x | x ∈ A ∧ Q x}, subj_intro 4.
sep_intro. ⊢ u ∈ A → Q u → u ∈ {x | x ∈ A ∧ Q x},
  subj_intro 5.

Dependencies
The given proof depends on one axiom:
separation.
