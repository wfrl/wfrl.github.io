Theorem K_lemma_iv

Theorem. K_lemma_iv
□(A → □A) → ¬◇(A ∧ ◇¬A)
Proof
01. 1 ⊢ A → □A, hypo.
02. 2 ⊢ A, hypo.
03. 1, 2 ⊢ □A, subj_elim 1 2.
04. 1, 2 ⊢ ¬◇¬A, K_lemma_i 3.
05. 1 ⊢ A → ¬◇¬A, subj_intro 4.
06. 1 ⊢ ¬(A ∧ ◇¬A), lemma_i 5.
07. ⊢ (A → □A) → ¬(A ∧ ◇¬A), subj_intro 6.
08. ⊢ □((A → □A) → ¬(A ∧ ◇¬A)), nec 7.
09. ⊢ □(A → □A) → □¬(A ∧ ◇¬A), K 8.
10. 10 ⊢ □(A → □A), hypo.
11. 10 ⊢ □¬(A ∧ ◇¬A), subj_elim 9 10.
12. 10 ⊢ ¬◇¬¬(A ∧ ◇¬A), K_lemma_i 11.
13. 10 ⊢ ¬◇(A ∧ ◇¬A), K_lemma_iii 12.
K_lemma_iv. ⊢ □(A → □A) → ¬◇(A ∧ ◇¬A),
  subj_intro 13.

Dependencies
The given proof depends on two axioms:
K, pos_equi.
