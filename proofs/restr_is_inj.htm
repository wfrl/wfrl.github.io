Theorem restr_is_inj

Theorem. restr_is_inj
inj f X Y → A ⊆ X → inj (restr f A) A Y
Proof
01. 1 ⊢ inj f X Y, hypo.
02. 2 ⊢ A ⊆ X, hypo.
03. 1 ⊢ map f X Y, inj_is_map 1.
04. 1, 2 ⊢ map (restr f A) A Y, map_restr 3 2.
05. 5 ⊢ a ∈ A, hypo.
06. 6 ⊢ b ∈ A, hypo.
07. 7 ⊢ app (restr f A) a = app (restr f A) b, hypo.
08. 1, 2, 5 ⊢ app f a = app (restr f A) a, app_restr 3 2 5.
09. 1, 2, 6 ⊢ app f b = app (restr f A) b, app_restr 3 2 6.
10. 1, 2, 5, 7 ⊢ app f a = app (restr f A) b, eq_trans 8 7.
11. 1, 2, 5, 6, 7 ⊢ app f a = app f b, eq_trans_rr 10 9.
12. 2, 5 ⊢ a ∈ X, incl_elim 2 5.
13. 2, 6 ⊢ b ∈ X, incl_elim 2 6.
14. 1, 2, 5, 6, 7 ⊢ a = b, inj_elim 1 12 13 11.
15. 1, 2 ⊢ a ∈ A → b ∈ A →
  app (restr f A) a = app (restr f A) b → a = b,
  subj_intro_iii 14.
16. 1, 2 ⊢ ∀b. a ∈ A → b ∈ A →
  app (restr f A) a = app (restr f A) b → a = b,
  uq_intro 15.
17. 1, 2 ⊢ ∀a. ∀b. a ∈ A → b ∈ A →
  app (restr f A) a = app (restr f A) b → a = b,
  uq_intro 16.
18. 1, 2 ⊢ inj (restr f A) A Y, inj_intro 4 17.
restr_is_inj. ⊢ inj f X Y → A ⊆ X →
  inj (restr f A) A Y, subj_intro_ii 18.
