Theorem. inv_elim
(y, x) ∈ inv R → (x, y) ∈ R
Proof
01. 1 ⊢ (y, x) ∈ inv R, hypo.
02. 1 ⊢ (y, x) ∈ {t | ∃a. ∃b. t = (b, a) ∧ (a, b) ∈ R},
  eq_subst inv_eq 1, P A ↔ (y, x) ∈ A.
03. 1 ⊢ ∃a. ∃b. (y, x) = (b, a) ∧ (a, b) ∈ R, comp_elim 2.
04. 4 ⊢ ∃b. (y, x) = (b, a) ∧ (a, b) ∈ R, hypo.
05. 5 ⊢ (y, x) = (b, a) ∧ (a, b) ∈ R, hypo.
06. 5 ⊢ (a, b) ∈ R, conj_elimr 5.
07. 5 ⊢ set (a, b), set_intro 6.
08. 5 ⊢ set a, setl_from_pair 7.
09. 5 ⊢ set b, setr_from_pair 7.
10. 5 ⊢ (y, x) = (b, a), conj_eliml 5.
11. 5 ⊢ (b, a) = (y, x), eq_symm 10.
12. 5 ⊢ b = y ∧ a = x, pair_property 9 8 11.
13. 5 ⊢ b = y, conj_eliml 12.
14. 5 ⊢ a = x, conj_elimr 12.
15. 5 ⊢ (x, b) ∈ R, eq_subst 14 6.
16. 5 ⊢ (x, y) ∈ R, eq_subst 13 15.
17. 4 ⊢ (x, y) ∈ R, ex_elim 4 16.
18. 1 ⊢ (x, y) ∈ R, ex_elim 3 17.
inv_elim. ⊢ (y, x) ∈ inv R → (x, y) ∈ R, subj_intro 18.
