Theorem ico_empty

Theorem. ico_empty
a ∈ ℝ → ico a a = ∅
Proof
01. 1 ⊢ a ∈ ℝ, hypo.
02. 2 ⊢ x ∈ ico a a, hypo.
03. 2 ⊢ x ∈ ℝ ∧ a ≤ x ∧ x < a, ico_elim 2.
04. 2 ⊢ x ∈ ℝ, conj_elimll 3.
05. 2 ⊢ a ≤ x, conj_elimlr 3.
06. 2 ⊢ x < a, conj_elimr 3.
07. 1, 2 ⊢ a < a, rle_lt_trans 1 4 1 5 6.
08. 1, 2 ⊢ ⊥, lt_self_contra 7.
09. 1, 2 ⊢ x ∈ ∅, efq 8.
10. 1 ⊢ x ∈ ico a a → x ∈ ∅, subj_intro 9.
11. 1 ⊢ ∀x. x ∈ ico a a → x ∈ ∅, uq_intro 10.
12. 1 ⊢ ico a a ⊆ ∅, incl_intro 11.
13. ⊢ ∅ ⊆ ico a a, empty_set_is_least.
14. 1 ⊢ ico a a = ∅, incl_antisym 12 13.
ico_empty. ⊢ a ∈ ℝ → ico a a = ∅, subj_intro 14.

Dependencies
The given proof depends on seven axioms:
comp, efq, eq_refl, eq_subst, ext, rle_antisym, rle_trans.
