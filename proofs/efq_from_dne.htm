Theorem efq_from_dne

Theorem. efq_from_dne
(¬¬A → A) → (⊥ → A)

States that ex falso quodlibet can be derived from double negation elimination.

Proof sketch
Let ¬¬A → A hold. To be shown is ⊥ → A, that is, A under the additional hypothesis ⊥. Then ⊥ holds even more so under the further hypothesis ¬A. Since ¬A leads to a contradiction in this respect, ¬¬A must hold. Ergo, A follows via the first hypothesis from this finding. q.e.d.

Proof
01. 1 ⊢ ¬¬A → A, hypo.
02. 2 ⊢ ⊥, hypo.
03. 3 ⊢ ¬A, hypo.
04. 2, 3 ⊢ ⊥, wk 2.
05. 2 ⊢ ¬¬A, neg_intro 4.
06. 1, 2 ⊢ A, subj_elim 1 5.
efq_from_dne. ⊢ (¬¬A → A) → (⊥ → A), subj_intro_ii 6.
