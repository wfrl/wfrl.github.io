Axiom equi_subst

Axiom. equi_subst
(A ↔ B) → P A → P B

The admissible substitution schema of logical equivalence. It is needed to be able to perform equivalent transformations in a general way. It is feasible to do without it, but this can make the proof more complicated. It can be avoided by applying the congruence laws of equivalence manually, see equi_cong_neg, equi_cong_conj, equi_cong_disj, equi_cong_subj, equi_cong_bij.
