Theorem. inj_union
inj f X1 Y1 → inj g X2 Y2 → X1 ∩ X2 = ∅ → Y1 ∩ Y2 = ∅ → inj (f ∪ g) (X1 ∪ X2) (Y1 ∪ Y2)
Proof
01. 1 ⊢ inj f X1 Y1, hypo.
02. 2 ⊢ inj g X2 Y2, hypo.
03. 3 ⊢ X1 ∩ X2 = ∅, hypo.
04. 4 ⊢ Y1 ∩ Y2 = ∅, hypo.
05. 1 ⊢ map f X1 Y1, inj_is_map 1.
06. 2 ⊢ map g X2 Y2, inj_is_map 2.
07. 1, 2, 3 ⊢ map (f ∪ g) (X1 ∪ X2) (Y1 ∪ Y2), map_union 5 6 3.
08. 8 ⊢ a ∈ X1 ∪ X2, hypo.
09. 9 ⊢ b ∈ X1 ∪ X2, hypo.
10. 10 ⊢ app (f ∪ g) a = app (f ∪ g) b, hypo.
11. 8 ⊢ a ∈ X1 ∨ a ∈ X2, union_elim 8.
12. 9 ⊢ b ∈ X1 ∨ b ∈ X2, union_elim 9.
13. 13 ⊢ a ∈ X1, hypo.
14. 14 ⊢ b ∈ X1, hypo.
15. 1, 2, 3, 13 ⊢ app (f ∪ g) a = app f a, map_app_unionl 5 7 13.
16. 1, 2, 3, 14 ⊢ app (f ∪ g) b = app f b, map_app_unionl 5 7 14.
17. 1, 2, 3, 10, 13 ⊢ app f a = app (f ∪ g) b, eq_trans_ll 15 10.
18. 1, 2, 3, 10, 13, 14 ⊢ app f a = app f b, eq_trans 17 16.
19. 1, 2, 3, 10, 13, 14 ⊢ a = b, inj_elim 1 13 14 18.
20. 20 ⊢ b ∈ X2, hypo.
21. 1, 2, 3, 20 ⊢ app (f ∪ g) b = app g b, map_app_unionr 6 7 20.
22. 1, 2, 3, 10, 13, 20 ⊢ app f a = app g b, eq_trans 17 21.
23. 1, 13 ⊢ app f a ∈ Y1, map_app_in_cod 5 13.
24. 2, 20 ⊢ app g b ∈ Y2, map_app_in_cod 6 20.
25. 1, 2, 3, 10, 13, 20 ⊢ app f a ∈ Y2,
  eq_subst_rev 22 24, P t ↔ t ∈ Y2.
26. 1, 2, 3, 4, 10, 13, 20 ⊢ ⊥, disjoint_property 4 23 25.
27. 1, 2, 3, 4, 10, 13, 20 ⊢ a = b, efq 26.
28. 1, 2, 3, 4, 9, 10, 13 ⊢ a = b, disj_elim 12 19 27.
29. 29 ⊢ a ∈ X2, hypo.
30. 30 ⊢ b ∈ X1, hypo.
31. 1, 2, 3, 29 ⊢ app (f ∪ g) a = app g a, map_app_unionr 6 7 29.
32. 1, 2, 3, 30 ⊢ app (f ∪ g) b = app f b, map_app_unionl 5 7 30.
33. 1, 2, 3, 10, 30 ⊢ app (f ∪ g) a = app f b, eq_trans 10 32.
34. 1, 2, 3, 10, 29, 30 ⊢ app f b = app g a, eq_trans_ll 33 31.
35. 1, 30 ⊢ app f b ∈ Y1, map_app_in_cod 5 30.
36. 2, 29 ⊢ app g a ∈ Y2, map_app_in_cod 6 29.
37. 1, 2, 3, 10, 29, 30 ⊢ app f b ∈ Y2,
  eq_subst_rev 34 36, P t ↔ t ∈ Y2.
38. 1, 2, 3, 4, 10, 29, 30 ⊢ ⊥, disjoint_property 4 35 37.
39. 1, 2, 3, 4, 10, 29, 30 ⊢ a = b, efq 38.
40. 40 ⊢ b ∈ X2, hypo.
41. 1, 2, 3, 40 ⊢ app (f ∪ g) b = app g b, map_app_unionr 6 7 40.
42. 1, 2, 3, 10, 29 ⊢ app g a = app (f ∪ g) b, eq_trans_ll 31 10.
43. 1, 2, 3, 10, 29, 40 ⊢ app g a = app g b, eq_trans 42 41.
44. 1, 2, 3, 10, 29, 40 ⊢ a = b, inj_elim 2 29 40 43.
45. 1, 2, 3, 4, 9, 10, 29 ⊢ a = b, disj_elim 12 39 44.
46. 1, 2, 3, 4, 8, 9, 10 ⊢ a = b, disj_elim 11 28 45.
47. 1, 2, 3, 4 ⊢ a ∈ X1 ∪ X2 → b ∈ X1 ∪ X2 →
  app (f ∪ g) a = app (f ∪ g) b → a = b, subj_intro_iii 46.
48. 1, 2, 3, 4 ⊢ ∀b. a ∈ X1 ∪ X2 → b ∈ X1 ∪ X2 →
  app (f ∪ g) a = app (f ∪ g) b → a = b, uq_intro 47.
49. 1, 2, 3, 4 ⊢ ∀a. ∀b. a ∈ X1 ∪ X2 → b ∈ X1 ∪ X2 →
  app (f ∪ g) a = app (f ∪ g) b → a = b, uq_intro 48.
50. 1, 2, 3, 4 ⊢ inj (f ∪ g) (X1 ∪ X2) (Y1 ∪ Y2), inj_intro 7 49.
inj_union. ⊢ inj f X1 Y1 → inj g X2 Y2 → X1 ∩ X2 = ∅ → Y1 ∩ Y2 = ∅ →
  inj (f ∪ g) (X1 ∪ X2) (Y1 ∪ Y2), subj_intro_iv 50.
