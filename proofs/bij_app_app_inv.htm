Theorem. bij_app_app_inv
bij f X Y → y ∈ Y → app f (app (inv f) y) = y
Proof
01. 1 ⊢ bij f X Y, hypo.
02. 2 ⊢ y ∈ Y, hypo.
03. 1 ⊢ map f X Y, bij_is_map 1.
04. 1 ⊢ map (inv f) Y X, inv_of_bij_is_map 1.
05. ⊢ app (inv f) y = app (inv f) y, eq_refl.
06. 1, 2 ⊢ (y, app (inv f) y) ∈ inv f, map_app_elim 4 2 5.
07. 1, 2 ⊢ (app (inv f) y, y) ∈ f, inv_elim 6.
08. 1, 2 ⊢ y = app f (app (inv f) y), map_app_intro 3 7.
09. 1, 2 ⊢ app f (app (inv f) y) = y, eq_symm 8.
bij_app_app_inv. ⊢ bij f X Y → y ∈ Y →
  app f (app (inv f) y) = y, subj_intro_ii 9.

Dependencies
The given proof depends on nine axioms:
comp, efq, eq_refl, eq_subst, ext, lem, pairing, subset, union.
