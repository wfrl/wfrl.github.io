Theorem inv_of_bij_is_map

Theorem. inv_of_bij_is_map
bij f X Y → map (inv f) Y X
Proof
01. 1 ⊢ bij f X Y, hypo.
02. 1 ⊢ map f X Y, bij_is_map 1.
03. 1 ⊢ f ⊆ X × Y, map_incl_in_prod 2.
04. ⊢ relation (inv f), inv_is_relation.

05. 5 ⊢ (y, x1) ∈ inv f, hypo.
06. 6 ⊢ (y, x2) ∈ inv f, hypo.
07. 5 ⊢ (x1, y) ∈ f, inv_elim 5.
08. 6 ⊢ (x2, y) ∈ f, inv_elim 6.
09. 1, 5 ⊢ (x1, y) ∈ X × Y, incl_elim 3 7.
10. 1, 6 ⊢ (x2, y) ∈ X × Y, incl_elim 3 8.
11. 1, 5 ⊢ x1 ∈ X ∧ y ∈ Y, prod_elim_pair 9.
12. 1, 6 ⊢ x2 ∈ X ∧ y ∈ Y, prod_elim_pair 10.
13. 1, 5 ⊢ x1 ∈ X, conj_eliml 11.
14. 1, 6 ⊢ x2 ∈ X, conj_eliml 12.
15. 1, 5 ⊢ y = app f x1, map_app_intro 2 7.
16. 1, 6 ⊢ y = app f x2, map_app_intro 2 8.
17. 1, 5, 6 ⊢ app f x1 = app f x2, eq_trans_ll 15 16.
18. 1 ⊢ inj f X Y, bij_is_inj 1.
19. 1, 5, 6 ⊢ x1 = x2, inj_elim 18 13 14 17.
20. 1 ⊢ (y, x1) ∈ inv f →
  (y, x2) ∈ inv f → x1 = x2, subj_intro_ii 19.
21. 1 ⊢ ∀x2. (y, x1) ∈ inv f →
  (y, x2) ∈ inv f → x1 = x2, uq_intro 20.
22. 1 ⊢ ∀x1. ∀x2. (y, x1) ∈ inv f →
  (y, x2) ∈ inv f → x1 = x2, uq_intro 21.
23. 1 ⊢ ∀y. ∀x1. ∀x2. (y, x1) ∈ inv f →
  (y, x2) ∈ inv f → x1 = x2, uq_intro 22.
24. 1 ⊢ function (inv f), function_intro 4 23.

25. ⊢ dom (inv f) = rng f, dom_inv.
26. 1 ⊢ sur f X Y, bij_is_sur 1.
27. 1 ⊢ rng f = Y, sur_elim 26.
28. 1 ⊢ dom (inv f) = Y, eq_trans 25 27.

29. ⊢ rng (inv f) = dom f, rng_inv.
30. 1 ⊢ dom f = X, map_dom 2.
31. 1 ⊢ rng (inv f) = X, eq_trans 29 30.
32. 1 ⊢ rng (inv f) ⊆ X, incl_from_eq 31.

33. 1 ⊢ map (inv f) Y X, map_intro 24 28 32.
inv_of_bij_is_map. ⊢ bij f X Y → map (inv f) Y X,
  subj_intro 33.
