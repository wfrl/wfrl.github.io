Definition. composition_eq
S ∘ R = {t | ∃x. ∃y. ∃z. t = (x, z) ∧ (x, y) ∈ R ∧ (y, z) ∈ S}

The composition of two relations S and R is the relation containing every pair (x, z) for which there exists a common element y such that (x, y) is an element of R and (y, z) is an element of S.
