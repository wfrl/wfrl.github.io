Theorem inv_is_relation

Theorem. inv_is_relation
relation (inv R)
Proof
01. 1 ⊢ t ∈ inv R, hypo.
02. 1 ⊢ t ∈ {t | ∃x. ∃y. t = (y, x) ∧ (x, y) ∈ R},
  eq_subst inv_eq 1, P A ↔ t ∈ A.
03. 1 ⊢ ∃x. ∃y. t = (y, x) ∧ (x, y) ∈ R, comp_elim 2.
04. 4 ⊢ ∃y. t = (y, x) ∧ (x, y) ∈ R, hypo.
05. 5 ⊢ t = (y, x) ∧ (x, y) ∈ R, hypo.
06. 5 ⊢ t = (y, x), conj_eliml 5.
07. 5 ⊢ ∃b. t = (y, b), ex_intro 6.
08. 5 ⊢ ∃a. ∃b. t = (a, b), ex_intro 7.
09. 4 ⊢ ∃a. ∃b. t = (a, b), ex_elim 4 8.
10. 1 ⊢ ∃a. ∃b. t = (a, b), ex_elim 3 9.
11. ⊢ t ∈ inv R → ∃a. ∃b. t = (a, b), subj_intro 10.
12. ⊢ ∀t. t ∈ inv R → ∃a. ∃b. t = (a, b), uq_intro 11.
inv_is_relation. ⊢ relation (inv R), relation_fold 12.

Dependencies
The given proof depends on two axioms:
comp, eq_subst.
